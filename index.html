Agri-Business Show 2020

Date: Oct 16, 2020

Time: 09:00 AM - 01:00 PM

The Region's Primier Virtual Agriculture Expo will be held from October 15-18, 2020 and will cover all segments of Agriculture, Animal Husbandry, Fishing, Dairy & Poultry. The main aim of this virtual expo is to highlight the scope and opportunities in three sectors post Covid-19 scenario. It will also focus on developing farm entrepreneurship, farm mechanization, agro processing and technology development.
